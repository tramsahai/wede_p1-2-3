Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my enquiry?

+

We typically respond to all enquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our helpline at +91 98765 43210.

What types of partnerships do you offer?

+

We offer various partnership opportunities including corporate sponsorships, program collaborations, employee volunteering, and cause-related marketing. Our partnership team will work with you to create a customized approach that aligns with your organization's goals.

Can I volunteer remotely or do I need to be in Bangladesh?

+

We offer both on-site and remote volunteering opportunities. Remote volunteers can help with digital marketing, content creation, research, and online mentoring. On-site volunteers work directly with our communities in Bangladesh.

Are donations to Shakti Foundation tax-deductible?

+

Yes, donations to Shakti Foundation are tax-deductible under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act. We provide donation receipts for all contributions that can be used for tax purposes.

How can my organization collaborate with Shakti Foundation?

+

We welcome collaborations with organizations through program funding, employee engagement, skills-based volunteering, and awareness campaigns. Please select "Partnership" as your enquiry type above, and our partnership team will contact you to discuss opportunities.
