Our Mission & History of Women Empowerment

Founded in 1992 in Dhaka, Bangladesh by Dr. Humaira Islam, Shakti Foundation emerged from a vision to create lasting change for underprivileged women through sustainable empowerment programs.

  • Microfinance and Economic Empowerment: Small business loans and financial literacy training
  • Healthcare Access and Medical Services: Mobile clinics and preventive care programs
  • Education and Scholarship Programs: Supporting children's education and adult literacy
  • Skills Training and Vocational Development: Marketable skills for employment
  • Community Advocacy and Support: Women's rights and community development
